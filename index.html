Respond to Reminders


  1. Live with optimism, generosity, & humble confidence.

  2. Love your neighbor as yourself - rejoice & do good.

  3. Love Brooke Always - delight & submit, pursue & protect.

  4. Don't take yourself too seriously.

  5. What will you do today to reach your goals tomorrow?

  6. What will you/others say about yourself/you 10/25/50 years from now?

  7. Don't base personal expectations on what you believe others are doing.

  8. Have foresight, but live in the moment.

  9. Gratitude determines attitude - make yours an attitude of optimism.

  10. Be quick to listn and slow to speak.
